## Summarizing The Results of Calculating Yields To Maturity

This chapter defined and calculated yields to maturity. For bonds that make single payments, yields to maturity are defined with a simple formula.  An iterative procedure is required for bonds with mu;tiple payments.  The Newton-Raphson technique is used to search for yields to maturity.  Yields to maturity provide insight to the relative pricing of bonds.  Bonds with the same coupon, maturity, and risk should sell for the same yield to maturity.  Although par yields are derived from the term structure and yields to maturity from specific bonds, the concepts are related.
